My Favourite movies and tv shows

  • Rocky: A small time Philidelphia boxer gets the chance to fight the world heavyweight champion
  • Interstellar: When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.
  • Megamind: When cunning supervillain Megamind accidently kills his crime-fighting nemesis, he creates a new enemy who seeks to destroy the world.
  • Zoolander: Three-time male model of the year, Derek Zoolander is roped into corporate espionage
  • Forrest Gump: The history of the United States from the 1950s to the '70s unfolds from the perspective of an Alabama man with an IQ of 75, who yearns to be reunited with his childhood sweetheart.
  • Oppenheimer: The story of the inventor of the atomic bomb
  • Breaking Bad: A Chemistry teacher does some less than savoury things in order to pay for his cancer treatment
  • Invincible: An animated series based on the Skybound/Image comic about a teenager whose father is the most powerful superhero on the planet.
  • Cowboy Bebop: The futuristic misadventures and tragedies of an easygoing bounty hunter and his partners.
  • The Office: A deep dive into the less than ordinary lives of people working for a paper company in Philidelphia
  • Community: A former lawyer attends a community college after faking his Bachelor's degree
  • Family Guy: The adventures of the Griffin family and the other residents of the town Quahog
